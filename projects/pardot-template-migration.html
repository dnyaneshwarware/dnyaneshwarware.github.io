Salesforce Pardot HTML/CSS Brand Compliance

Migration of Legacy Pardot Layout Templates to BNY Branded Standards

Impact: Achieved 100% brand consistency and optimized cross-device email deliverability.

Managed the end-to-end migration of dozens of legacy email layout templates to a new, unified BNY-branded framework. This project focused on improving the technical delivery and visual identity of global marketing campaigns.

The Challenges

  • Technical Debt: Existing templates were outdated, non-responsive (fixed width), and incompatible with modern mobile mail clients.
  • Brand Risk: Non-compliant templates created inconsistent brand experiences across different marketing regions.
  • Operational Pressure: Required a seamless migration with zero disruption to active, high-volume email campaign schedules.

The Strategic Solution

I took ownership of the transition by combining technical redesign with strategic brand auditing:

  • Audit & Mapping: Analyzed the template library to map legacy assets to new enterprise brand standards.
  • Responsive Development: Engineered new templates using mobile-first HTML/CSS to ensure perfect rendering across Outlook, Gmail, and mobile devices.
  • Advanced Personalization: Implemented dynamic content blocks and Handlebars Merge Language (HML) to scale 1-to-1 personalization.
  • Rigorous Testing: Utilized rendering tools to verify deliverability, spam compliance, and visual accuracy across 50+ email clients.

Impact & Results

  • 100% Compliance: Successfully rolled out a fully branded template library globally without any campaign downtime.
  • Engagement Uplift: Observed a measurable increase in email open rates and CTR due to modern, accessible design principles.
  • Efficiency Gains: Reduced future template maintenance and creation time through a modular, "building block" approach.
