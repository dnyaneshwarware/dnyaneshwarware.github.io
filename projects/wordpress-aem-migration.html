Adobe AEM WordPress API Power Query

WordPress to Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) Migration

Impact: Reduced Content Freeze by 75% and accelerated migration timeline.

Led the end-to-end migration of a high-volume WordPress site to Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) to enable scalable, compliant, and personalized content delivery at BNY Mellon.

The Challenge

  • Scale: Migrating massive volumes of post-type data while ensuring full metadata integrity.
  • Business Continuity: Standard migration practices often require month-long "content freezes," halting marketing agility.
  • Compliance: Required a secure backup and audit trail for all legacy posts and revisions to meet strict regulatory requirements.

The Strategic Solution

I engineered a data-driven migration pipeline that prioritized real-time updates over static exports:

  • Data Extraction: Utilized the WordPress API and XML exports to pull structured post data programmatically.
  • Transformation: Leveraged Microsoft Power Query to cleanse and map legacy metadata to AEM-compatible schemas.
  • Feeds Automation: Implemented real-time feeds of recently modified posts, allowing the development team to prioritize live updates.
  • Audit Readiness: Designed a secure backup database of all historical posts to ensure full compliance during and after the transition.

Impact & Results

  • 75% Efficiency Gain: Reduced the mandatory content freeze period from 1 month to just 1 week.
  • Zero Data Loss: Successfully migrated all assets with full audit compliance and zero corruption.
  • Marketing Agility: Enabled continuous content updates during the transition, preventing gaps in market-facing communications.
